HW 2: In this project I learned how to display images in different projections

I used a shape file from Natural Earth (linked below) as the base map, and then used a different EPSG code for each base map with the Indacatrix Mapper Plug-In.

WGS84 Projection

This map becomes distorted the farther you go away from the Equator.

Aitoff Projection

This map is most accurate around the water near Nigeria.

Pseudo Mercator

The sizes / area of the continents, especially the poles, is heavily distorted.

Sphere Winkel

The poles both seem to be heavily distorted.

World Cylindrical Equal Area

The poles seem very "squashed" and distorted although distance / area seems to be more accurate.

World Equidistant Conic

This map heavily expands the area the farther and farther you get from the North Pole.

North Pole Azimuthal Equidistant

This map is similar to the previous one, with size being distorted more and more the farther one is from the North Pole.

World Mollweide

All parts of this map seem to have the area distorted.

Asia North Albers Equal Area Conic

This map seeks to keep Europe the most accurate, while distorting and even eliminating some parts of the globe.
